Rice Production

Key Insights

  • Indonesia leads ASEAN in rice production with 163 million tons annually.
  • Vietnam and Thailand follow closely, together accounting for 60% of regional exports.
  • Smaller nations like Timor-Leste and Brunei produce less than 1 million tons combined.

Key Insights

  • Philippines imports the most rice (12.6M tons), accounting for 45% of regional imports.
  • Malaysia follows with 5.8M tons despite its smaller population size.
  • Traditional producers like Thailand and Vietnam still import for specific rice varieties.

Key Insights

  • Thailand and Vietnam dominate exports with over 25M tons each annually.
  • Cambodia has emerged as a significant exporter with 3.2M tons.
  • Most ASEAN countries export some rice, except for Philippines and Indonesia.
